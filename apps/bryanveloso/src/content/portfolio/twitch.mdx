At Twitch, I worked as a UI/UX Designer during a pivotal growth period as the platform expanded beyond gaming into broader content categories and was acquired by Amazon.

## Key Contributions

- Designed core user interface components for Twitch's web and desktop applications
- Improved the streaming experience for both content creators and viewers
- Collaborated on design systems that maintained consistency across Twitch's rapidly evolving product
- Created interactive prototypes to test new features and user flows
- Worked closely with engineering and product teams to implement designs at scale

## Impact

My design work at Twitch helped shape how millions of viewers interact with livestreaming content daily. I contributed to the platform's evolution during a period of significant growth and change, focusing on creating intuitive interfaces that served both casual viewers and power users in the streaming community.
